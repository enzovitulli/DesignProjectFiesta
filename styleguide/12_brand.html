Brand Guidelines

Logo Usage

Correct: Original sprite animation on hover

Incorrect: Do not resize or distort the sprite

// Logo Sprite Animation
.logo img {
    height: 4rem;
    width: 4rem;
    object-fit: none;
    object-position: 0 0;
    filter: brightness(0) invert(1);
}

Brand Voice

Tone of Voice

  • Premium and sophisticated
  • Energetic and vibrant
  • Modern and urban
  • Inclusive and welcoming

Writing Style

  • Use active voice
  • Short, impactful sentences
  • Emphasize experiences and emotions
  • Maintain professionalism while being approachable
